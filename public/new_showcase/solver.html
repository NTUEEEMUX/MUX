Multiplexer Showcase

How to use:


• Choose the desired multiplexer and fill in the inputs & selector(s) configurations.

• After submission, the min and maxterms would be returned, along with outputs at each term iteration.


Additional Informations:


• Supported multiplexers can be selected via the dropdown.

• variables supported ranges from A to F (including binaries).

• Use "(" and ")" to denote piority functions.

• Avalible logics can be input using the buttons provided below.


Sample input:

• C ⊖ (A • (B + C))' ⊕ D
